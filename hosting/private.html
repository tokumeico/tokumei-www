Host your own private Tokumei site

Tor

Create a Tor hidden service. This protects your anonymity as the site's host and prevents censorship, at the cost of making your site more difficult to access.

We recommend Tor for better anonymity and free speech.

Clearnet

Create a clearnet site. This does not protect your anonymity as the site's host and makes your site vulnerable to censorship, but makes your site easier for users to access.

We recommend Tor over the clearnet for its better anonymity and free speech.

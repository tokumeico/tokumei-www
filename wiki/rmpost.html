Deleting posts

  • Get the post ID

    • Visit your site, click on the post's menu button and then Share. Note the number at the end of the post URL.

  • Delete the post

    • Run the following, replacing 123 with the offending post ID.

    PATH=$PATH:/usr/lib/plan9/bin ./bin/aux/rmpost.rc 123
